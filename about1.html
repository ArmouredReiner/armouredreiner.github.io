What To Do After a Crash

Don't know what to do? Ring us and we'll get you back on the road

When you’ve been involved in an accident, having the correct information ready helps speed up the repair and insurance process. But whether you have the information or not we will help you get back on the road.

If possible you can bring the following:

  • Your driver’s licence
  • Your vehicle registration
  • The other party’s licence details
  • Their vehicle details
  • Any photos or videos of the crash

The more information you provide, the faster we can assist in processing your claim.

Pulling & Structural Alignment

Once your vehicle arrives in our workshop, we follow a detailed and proven repair procedure to ensure your car is restored to factory condition.

From initial assessment to OEM parts installation, structural alignment, painting, and final checks — every step is done with precision.

  • Initial Assessment – full damage inspection
  • Strip Down – remove damaged components
  • Parts Replacement – genuine OEM parts fitted
  • Chassis Alignment – gaps checked and corrected
  • Painting – colour matching and blending
  • Reassembly – final fit and quality checks

Car Hire Assistance

While your vehicle is being repaired, we provide car hire options so you can continue your day-to-day life without interruption.

Our rentals are tailored for insurance claims and we handle most paperwork on your behalf.

  • Replacement cars for insurance claims
  • Minimal paperwork
  • Flexible rental durations
  • We communicate with insurers for you

Car Write-Off Assistance

If your vehicle is classified as a write-off, we can assist you throughout the entire process.

We make sure you receive your full entitlement and understand every step of the process.

Our team works directly with insurers and assessors to make the process fast and stress-free.
