Dent Repairs

From car park dings to hail damage, our dent repair specialists restore your vehicle's body to a flawless finish. Using both traditional techniques and modern paintless dent repair methods, we offer solutions for every type of dent damage.

Paintless Dent Repair (PDR)

Remove dents without touching your original paintwork using specialised tools and techniques. Ideal for car park dings, hail damage, and minor collisions.

Traditional Dent Repair

For dents that can't be repaired with PDR, we use filler, sanding, and repainting techniques to achieve a seamless, invisible repair.

Hail Damage Repair

Specialised PDR techniques to remove hail dents efficiently, preserving your vehicle's original paint and value.

Door Ding Repair

Quick and effective removal of door dings and minor dents caused by shopping carts, mall doors, and other everyday incidents.

Panel Alignment

Precision panel realignment to correct misaligned panels that create gaps or affect how doors and hoods close.

Scratch & Chip Repair

Expert touch-up and repair of scratches, stone chips, and paint imperfections to restore your car's finish.
